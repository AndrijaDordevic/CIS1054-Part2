About Us

Welcome to Baked Gold

At Baked Gold, we believe that every bite should be a delightful experience. Nestled in the heart of Mellieha, our bakery is a haven for those who cherish the art of baking and the joy of indulging in freshly made treats.

Our Story

Baked Gold was founded in 2024 by John Hoiles, a passionate baker with a dream to create a community space where people could come together over delicious, handcrafted baked goods. What started as a small, family-run business has blossomed into a beloved local favorite, known for its warm atmosphere and exceptional products.

Our Philosophy

Quality and tradition are at the heart of everything we do. We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and every item is baked fresh daily. From our rich, buttery croissants to our decadent chocolate cakes, each creation is made with love and meticulous attention to detail.

Our Products

Our diverse menu features a wide array of baked goods, catering to all tastes and dietary preferences. Whether you're in the mood for a classic pastry, a savory quiche, or a custom-designed cake for a special occasion, we have something to satisfy your cravings. We also offer a selection of gluten-free and vegan options, ensuring everyone can enjoy a taste of Sweet Bliss.

Our Commitment

At Baker Name, we are committed to sustainability and supporting our local community. We strive to reduce our environmental footprint by using eco-friendly packaging and minimizing waste. Additionally, we partner with local farmers and suppliers to ensure our ingredients are of the highest quality and ethically sourced.

Visit Us

Step into Baker Name and let the aroma of freshly baked goods draw you in. Our friendly team is always here to greet you with a smile and help you find the perfect treat. Whether you're picking up a quick breakfast, enjoying a leisurely coffee break, or celebrating a special moment, Sweet Bliss Bakery is your go-to destination for all things delicious.

Thank you for being a part of our story. We look forward to serving you.
